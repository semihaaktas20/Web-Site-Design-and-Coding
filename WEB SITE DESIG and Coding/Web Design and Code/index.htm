Our Story

Shine Cakes Patisserie

The founding chef of our patisserie set out to realize her pastry dream. Our chef, who completed her pastry education, started to work by making desserts for restaurants. Our chef opened our patisserie in Istanbul. Our patisserie currently has one branch. We work with our team employees in branch. We serve in our patisserie with many of our chefs and staff. In addition, we make special designs concept cakes according to the wishes of our customers. Would you like to try delicious cakes and desserts prepared by our chefs with their unique designs?
